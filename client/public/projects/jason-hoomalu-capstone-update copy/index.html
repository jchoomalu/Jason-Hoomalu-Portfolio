Humans

Arrogant, Marvelous.

We knew we were killing the planet. Preaching to the masses about global warming and carbon emissions, all the while pumping enormous amounts of pollution into the atmosphere.

INDUSTRY

Corporate Control

We reach the point of no return. Humans begin to evolve, children are born with unusual abilities. The global corporation INDUSTRY realize the increased levels of co2 are the cause. In an attempt to harness the amazing power of the evolution, INDUSTRY begins intentionally releasing co2 into the atmosphere. The world's governments try to intervene, INDUSTRY is too strong.

War

Mass Destruction

War rages, starting in the West, INDUSTRY cuts off the fuel, electricity, and water supplies, agriculture fails. The people are divided, soldiers turn on their brothers for greed, children are abandoned because of their strange abilities, all forms of government crumble. INDUSTRY uses their resources to create highly evolved human weapons. It seems the radiation levels from the war increase the rate of evolution.

INDUSTRY Reigns

Humanity's Foothold

Over thousands of years INDUSTRY continues to destroy the planet. They have found ways to speed up the evolution, poisoning the planet further in the process. Yet, every generation is born stronger with more natural abilities. A group of like-minded, highly evolved people band together to form the group known as SYMBOL.

And they have decided to fight back...
